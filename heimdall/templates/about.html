About Heimdallr

We are Ragnarök Industries and we are developing the Heimdallr smart doorbell. Chelsea Miller is our project manager, with Heidi Mosen, Jamie Sanson, and Grayson Hughes as the project team.

The Heimdallr smart doorbell comprises of a compact, waterproof case housing an Arduino Microcontroller. The case design is as minimal as possible, consisting of just a simple button to lead to short user interactions.

Ragnarok is the Norse end of days. It’s a period of time were the old gods are reborn and as is human civilization. We’re seeing this as a fresh start, a change to something completely different. Here we had the idea of a basic doorbell, and flipped it on its head. What we have now is an idea for something completely new. It’s a mix of security and convenience, and we call it Heimdallr.
